MEET OUR TEAM

Meet Onubogu Uchechukwu , a visionary tech enthusiast and the founder of MDTF (Making A Difference Tech Fest), who has carved a unique path by blending her background in dental technology with a passion for user experience and user interface (UX/UI) design.

In founding MDTF, Uchechukwu sought to create a platform that champions multi-disciplinary collaboration, fostering connections between professionals from various backgrounds, all united by their shared enthusiasm for technology. MDTF serves as a hub for tech enthusiasts, designers, and innovators, promoting the exchange of ideas, skills, and knowledge to push the boundaries of what's possible in the digital world.
